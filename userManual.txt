• When you are using the ATM, stand in front of the machine to be
able to cover the ATM screen, keypad and cash dispenser. This is
important for security.
• The home page of our atm has an input entry label to grab code (pin)
• After validation, the next menu allow to choose the account you want to use.
 you can choose between savings and checking account
• For either choose, a menu with withdraw ,deposit and balance buttons will  be display.
   • For withdrawal , click on the withdraw buttom , then select or enter the amount manually.
   • For deposit , click on the deposit buttom , then enter the amount manually.
   • For checking your balance, click on the balance button.
